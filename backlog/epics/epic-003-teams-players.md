# Epic 003 — Teams & Players

**Status:** In backlog  
**Roadmap phase:** Teams & Players  
**Actors:** Coach, Player, Admin

## Goal

Coaches can create and manage teams and rosters. Players can create and maintain personal profiles. Players can find teams to join.
