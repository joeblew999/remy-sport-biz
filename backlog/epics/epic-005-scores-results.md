# Epic 005 — Scores & Results

**Status:** In backlog  
**Roadmap phase:** Scores & Results  
**Actors:** Organizer, Referee, Admin (write) · All (read)  
**Event types:** Tournament, League, Showcase (scores) · Camp/Clinic (attendance)

---

## Goal

Live and historical results are available to everyone. Organizers and referees can enter scores and confirm match status. Players and spectators can hide scores with spoiler mode.
